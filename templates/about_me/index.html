Who I am

My name is Karen Rustad. I was born in Sunnyvale, CA; grew up in Edina, MN; graduated from Scripps College in Claremont, CA in 2008 with a degree in Media Studies; lived after college in Arlington, VA; moved to Atlanta, GA to work with OpenHatch, a startup in the Shotput Ventures summer incubator program; moved to Philadelphia, PA to continue working with OpenHatch after the incubator finished; moved home to Edina, MN after the startup couldn't pay me anymore; and then moved to Berkeley, CA for grad school. I currently live in Oakland.

Hopefully I'll stay here in the Bay Area for a while.

In 2012 I graduated with a master's degree from UC Berkeley's School of Information (an odd but fun combination of computer science, UI design, library science, sociology, and management).

My personal hobbies and interests include meteorology, gender politics, intellectual property and technology law and policy, baby naming trends, science fiction, Indian cooking, webcomics, and rock climbing.
